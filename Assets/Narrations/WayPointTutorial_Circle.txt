Welcome to the way point direction tutorial.<br>
In this session, I will teach you what is way point, and how to identify the direction of a way point.<br>
A way point is an intermediate point on a route. You can imagine it as a stop on a bus line. Several consecutive way points make up a route.<br>

In a game, a drum beat 3D music will be played from the active way point. It sounds like: <br>
Since it's 3D music. In a game, you can figure out the direction where the music comes from.<br>
When you hear the drum beat music, try to rotate your head to your left, and then to your right. <br>
By doing this, you will hear the volume of music in your left and right ear changes.<br>
After that, try to turn to the direction where the volume of the music is higher.<br>
When your face is facing toward the direction of music, both of your ears will hear music of the same volume. <br>
At the same time, the drum beat music will become very fast. It will sound like: <br>

Once you find it, keep your face facing towards that direction for 1 second to confirm. <br>
